Feria Gijon, Northern Spain, 11 - 25 August 2004

Click on images to download larger version.

Returning to Gijon a year later, we presented a show sponsored by Hydro Cantabrico, the local power utility. On this occasion half the building was completely blacked out with every surface black, black carpet on the floor, the ceiling painted black and the walls draped in thick black curtains. Visitors passed through a series of increasingly darkened spaces before arriving at the installation of kinetic light sculptures. The show was very popular with crowds lining up outside waiting to be allowed in.

The light sculptures were suspended from the ceiling at a height of 6 metres. You can see the artist taking a look at the installation from close up in some of these images. Sadly the crowds were so big that the public had to watch the installation from behind a handrail.
