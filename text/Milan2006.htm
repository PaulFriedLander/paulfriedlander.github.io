Milan Design Week April 2006

At 15 metres tall,

the largest light sculpture created to date.

Situated in the Porta Ticanese, at the Piazza 23 Maggio.

This project created in association with the lighting company Kundalini and the table water company, San Pelegrino.

The sculpture was lit by a ever changing abstract images generated in real time by computer and projected by two high power video projectors.
