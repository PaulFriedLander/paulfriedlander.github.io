Latymer Upper School

Latymer Upper School, in Hammersmith, West London. Commission for their new science building. The installation, Enigma of Light II was completed 31st May 2011.

Viewers interact via touch screen to influence the beams of light.

The brief called for the sculpture to fit within the pre existing glass cabinet designed by the architect.

Changing beams of light pass tangentially across the space and through prisms and lens like forms exploring and revealing their chromatics.
