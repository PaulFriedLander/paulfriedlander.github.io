Web Sites on Related Topics

http://www.yuart.com The ultimate directory for art on the web? Perhaps so, incredibly useful for artists, teachers or anyone interested in finding more art on the web.

This kids directory site includes, amongst many other things, links to web art and computer generated art.

www.cabuenes.org/arenas The Arenas Movedizas festival is a new fertival held annually in Gijon in northern Spain organised by FiumFoto. The festival is chiefly concerned with new media and collaborations by young Spanish artists.

www.olats.org/schoffer Nicholas Schoffer was one of the founders of cybernetic art. He came to prominence in the fifties. I saw his work first at the Kinetics Exhibition at the Hayward Gallery in 1970. It was inspiring. His work contributed strongly to my decision to become a light sculptor and was a major influence on me.

www.seeingwithsound.com Transform images into sound, primarily intended as an aid to the blind, but with great potential as an artistic tool.

www.artrock.com.fr Organisers of an annual festival held in St Brieuc, Brittany, France.

www.sonicacts.com Organisers of Sonic Light 2003, held in Amsterdam. They are also going to publish a book about this event.

www.kinetic-art.org The only international organisation entirely devoted to kinetc art.

www.artfutura.org The independent organisation that curates and runs artFutura. This is one of the most important festivals of hitech art, held annually in Barcelona.

www.sci-ctr.edu.sg The Singapore Science Centre also includes a vast omnimax cinema.

www.gg-designworld.com Greg Gutbezahl the designer I collaborated with to create The Energy Core.

Michael Millivolte Millivolte has developed luminous kinetic art works using spinning structures and ultra violet light: check it out, its cool.

Digital Summer Manchester's leading organisation bringing digital art to the region.

Nutopia The world's first Cyberlounge has opened in Covent Garden, London.

Photocells Light is not just beautiful, it is also practical. As well as the sun providing the energy for life on this planet, it can be harnessed to create electricity in an environmentally friendly way.

ASCI, Art and Science Collaboration, Inc, the collaborative organisation for artists and scientists on the East coast, organisers of Lightforms' 98.

New York Hall of Science New York's science museum and hosts for the Lightforms '98 exhibition.

Exploratorium San Francisco's famous hands on science museum.

Virtual Acid Psychedelia is alive on the web. The site if you are crazy about gif animations.

Eye Candy I have not checked this out but it could be a treat.

Ray Tracing Hundreds of sample images and expert advice on programming ray tracing.

YLEM An organisation for artist using science, based in the San Fancisco Bay area.

Holodeck 3 No prizes for guessing that this is a 'Trecky' site. I like the cool graphics.

Project Cool Cool guide to eclectic Web sites.
