Light Exhibition, phaeno science centre, Wolfsburg, Germany

Opened 28 September 2012 for 6 months

The light sculpture Persistence of Vision has a commanding presence at the approach to the show.

I am also showing the small Origin of String Theory. Interactivity is provided by touchscreen for both sculptures. In addition Origin of String Theory has the hands on approach to change the display.
