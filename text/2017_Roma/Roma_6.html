Art Futura Roma 2017

 

A child of the space age, I dreamed of building my own spaceship, and flying off alone. When I had grown art became a spaceship for my imagination and now I invite you to join me on my journey. Ever since Galileo championed the heresy the earth moved, the World at large has come to know spin as a character of all celestial bodies. Surprisingly even today spin continues to confound and cause controversy. The universe might be gently turning; the way galaxies rotate implies our understanding of gravity might be wrong. Even the spin of the tiny humble electron remains enigmatic. In Spinning Cosmos you will enter a space time machine to meditate upon these ideas.
