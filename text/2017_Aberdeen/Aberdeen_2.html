Aberdeen 2017

 

Not all a smooth ride, the first time I have set up a light installation outdoors in winter, Spectra Festival ran from 9-12 February. There was a worrying moment when I first switched on the lights, they did not light up. The cold and the damp were upsetting the electronics. All the equipment had to be hurriedly dismantled and brought into the warmth of the site porta cabin where happily it started to work again shortly after.
